WELCOME TO OUR SCHOOL

JOHANN-STEINGRUBER-SCHULE

It’s a secondary school in Bayern for over 800 students in there!

There are lots of different classes, including mathematician class, second-language class and an economical class, where students have the opportunity to fully open their potential!

The school offers a wide range of extracurricular activities, including sports teams, music clubs, and art classes. These activities provide students with opportunities to explore their interests and develop new skills outside of the classroom.

MUSIC EVENTS

Every year there are always 2 music “festivals” at school.

The main one is in December, before Christmas Eve. Students from the senior classes prepare with their teachers or with the music band, also located at school, the song they will be performing for the whole school!

And the second one takes place in the end of July, during the ceremony of graduation of the senior classes. All songs are sung by the students from the music band in order to show, what they achieved during their stay at school!

EDUCATION

At the end of this school student gets so called “mittlere - Reife” certificate.

This document opens the wide spectrum of opportunities for students in the future, for instance, student can go into so called FOS or BOS for the further education, in order to get into the University.

Don’t want to study? It is also not a problem. You can immediately work, if you desire to make some experience and money.
